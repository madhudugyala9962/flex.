Black Clover

Black Clover is a popular fantasy shōnen series following Asta, an orphan born without magic in a world where magic is everything. Armed with a rare "anti-magic" sword and unbreakable physical strength, he and his rival, Yuno—a prodigy mage—compete to become the next Wizard King of the Clover Kingdom.
